Our History




Founded in 2005, Sushi Japan has grown from a small neighborhood sushi bar to a beloved restaurant known for authentic flavors and creative presentations. Our founders dreamed of sharing the art of sushi with the local community and bringing a taste of Japan to every customer.






Over the years, we have expanded our menu, refined our techniques, and built relationships with trusted suppliers. Sustainability, quality, and innovation guide every decision we make. Today, Sushi Japan is recognized for its dedication to culinary excellence and for creating a welcoming space where food lovers can gather and enjoy genuine Japanese cuisine.
